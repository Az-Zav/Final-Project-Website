Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I place an order?

Browse our products and click "Shop Now" or "Add to Cart". Proceed to checkout and follow the payment instructions.

2. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, GCash, PayMaya, and bank transfers. Payment instructions are provided at checkout.

3. How long does delivery take?

Delivery usually takes 3-5 business days for Metro Manila and 5-10 business days for provincial areas.

4. Can I return or exchange a product?

Yes. Products must be unused, in original packaging, and returned within 7 days of delivery. See our Returns & Exchanges policy for details.

5. How do I track my order?

After your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email or SMS. Use it on the courier’s website to track your package.
