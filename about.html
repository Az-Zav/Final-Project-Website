The AeroKicks Story

Born from passion, crafted for the driven. We are proudly Filipino.

Our Purpose

Our Mission

To empower every Filipino to move confidently—one step at a time—through accessible, high-quality, and stylish footwear built for both lifestyle and performance.

Our Vision

To become the Philippines' most trusted homegrown sneaker brand, recognized across Asia for innovation, sustainability, and the celebration of Filipino identity.

From Enthusiasts to Innovators

Founded in 2029, AeroKicks PH was born from a passion for both streetwear culture and athletic performance. What started as a small online venture among sneaker enthusiasts has evolved into one of the Philippines' emerging lifestyle brands — merging comfort, creativity, and confidence in every pair.

We believe that footwear is more than just fashion — it's a statement of energy, movement, and individuality. That's why our designs are inspired by the pulse of city life, the grit of competition, and the soul of Filipino craftsmanship. Every stitch and material choice reflects our commitment to bringing premium, performance-driven gear to the local market.

Our journey is fueled by the desire to innovate while remaining authentic to our roots. We are constantly seeking sustainable materials and pioneering ergonomic designs to give our wearers an edge, whether they are on the court or conquering the urban landscape.
